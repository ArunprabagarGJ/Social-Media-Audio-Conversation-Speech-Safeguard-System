Speech Safeguard System

Abstract

The "Social Media Audio Conversations Speech Safeguard System" is a framework designed to improve security, privacy, and responsible audio-based interactions on social media platforms. It uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) algorithms to ensure accurate interpretation and protection of audio content. The system aims to create a secure and respectful environment for users, promoting responsible behavior and trust among platform users. It also enforces content moderation policies and ensures data security.

Introduction

The rise in audio conversations on social media platforms has raised concerns about privacy and security. The widespread availability of audio content on platforms introduces vulnerabilities such as privacy breaches, content misuse, and potential harm. Innovative solutions, such as the integration of Natural Language Processing (NLP) algorithms, have emerged to mitigate these challenges and uphold user privacy. NLP algorithms ensure accurate interpretation and safeguarding of audio content, empowering users with enhanced control over their interactions. This proactive approach also facilitates content moderation and policy enforcement, promoting responsible user behavior and safeguarding digital integrity.

Objective

This research aims to create a comprehensive Social Media Audio Conversations Speech Safeguard System using advanced technologies and methodologies. The system uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) algorithms to create a protective shield around audio content, preventing potential breaches. It also proactively detects and mitigates unauthorized sharing or misuse of audio content, upholding confidentiality. The system fosters a culture of responsible usage among users, promoting a safer digital landscape. It also enhances user confidence in the security of their audio content through transparent communication channels.

Proposed System

Enhancing the security and integrity of audio discussions on social media platforms is the goal of the suggested technology. To accurately translate speech inputs into text, it makes use of a Speech Recognition Module and a Bad Word Filter method. Additionally, the system has an Alert System that notifies users instantly when offensive language is discovered, encouraging responsibility and accountability.
